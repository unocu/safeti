What is SAFETI?

The SAFE, reimagined
for the token era

SAFE

Simple Agreement for Future Equity. Identical to YCombinator's SAFE — your investment converts to equity at a valuation cap or discount when a priced round occurs.

Token

Simple Agreement for Future Tokens. Your investment converts to cryptocurrency tokens at a future token generation event — ideal for crypto and web3 startups.

Interest

Simple Agreement for Future Interest. A multiple rate return on investment with an interest rate fallback — a clean, defined exit for investors and founders alike.

Unlike YCombinator's SAFE which only covers equity, SAFETI covers equity, tokens, AND interest — in a single document. One agreement, three investment paths.
